This notice explains how we process your personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). We act as the "Data Controller" for the information you provide via this website.

2. Legal Basis for Processing

Under the GDPR, we only process your data if we have a valid legal reason to do so. These typically include:

  • Consent: You have given us clear permission (e.g., signing up for a newsletter).
  • Contract: Processing is necessary to fulfill an agreement with you (e.g., delivering a product).
  • Legitimate Interests: Necessary for our business (e.g., analyzing website traffic to improve security), provided it doesn't override your rights.
  • Legal Obligation: We are required by law to keep the data (e.g., for tax purposes).

6. Your Rights (The "Big Eight")

As a resident of the EU/EEA, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your data.
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data.
  • Right to Erasure: Ask us to delete your data ("Right to be Forgotten").
  • Right to Restrict Processing: "Pause" how we use your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Get your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: Stop us from using data for direct marketing.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: At any time.
  • Rights on Automated Decision-Making: Protection against purely AI-driven decisions with legal effects.

7. Complaints

If you feel we haven't addressed your concerns, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local Data Protection Authority (DPA).
